# Code of conduct

We want this to be a kind, patient place for artists and developers of every experience level.

- Be respectful and specific when giving feedback.
- Welcome beginner questions and explain unfamiliar terms.
- Critique the contribution, never the contributor.
- Do not harass, threaten, discriminate, sexualize contributors, or publish private information.
- Respect creators' licenses, attribution, and requests to remove work.

Project maintainers may edit or remove abusive content and temporarily or permanently restrict
participation. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through the contact
method in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Good-faith reports will be handled discreetly.
