A tasteful chibi pixel fan depiction of Makise Kurisu: long auburn-red hair past the waist with
side-swept bangs, cool violet-blue eyes, tan-brown belted jacket over a white shirt and narrow black
tie, black shorts, opaque black thigh-high stockings, and brown ankle boots. Her confident,
analytical expression, costume, hair length, palette, proportions, and silhouette remain stable.

## Rendering

- Crisp hand-pixeled 16-bit adventure-game sprite aesthetic and limited warm palette.
- Chunky compact silhouette, dark stepped outline, flat cel shading, and no antialiasing.
- Tasteful, non-sexualized fan depiction with no logos, captions, or copied franchise art.
- Designed to remain readable inside a `192x208` cell.
- Generated on a flat `#00FF00` key, then deterministically extracted and despilled.

## State constraints

Rows follow `docs/PET_FORMAT.md`. No text, scenery, grid, cast shadow, glow, detached icons, speed
lines, dust, props, or neighboring-pose overlap. Active work is a focused analytical pose rather
than literal running. Look directions rotate gaze and head mechanics clockwise without rotating
the whole body.
