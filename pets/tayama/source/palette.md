# Palette notes

- Hair and eyes: burgundy red, wine shadows, muted rose highlights, deep red irises.
- Jacket and outline: midnight navy, charcoal, near-black, restrained brass studs and zippers.
- Dress and boots: pale mint-white, cool gray shadow, warm off-white highlights.
- Tights and skin: smoky brown, warm tan, muted peach.
- Supermarket vignette: cool concrete and steel grays, dark blue service panel, amber-yellow crate,
  warm fluorescent lamp.
- Cigarette: off-white paper, charcoal tip, tiny orange ember, pale neutral-gray smoke.
- Generation chroma key: bright green, excluded from every visible character and scene color and
  removed during final edge cleanup.
